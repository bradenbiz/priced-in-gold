Edge Cases

These should NOT be converted (they're not prices or are written-out amounts):

  • Year: 2024
  • Quantity: 5 items
  • Phone: (555) 123-4567
  • Date: 12/25/2024
  • Version: 1.2.3
  • Coordinates: 40.7128, -74.0060
  • Text descriptors: $500 hundred, $1 thousand dollars

These SHOULD be converted:

  • Cost: $0.99
  • Price: $10,000.00
  • Fee: USD 1.50
  • Amount: $1,234.56
  • Simple prices: $25, $1,299.99
  • Long price: $37,885,436,585,424.63
  • Written amounts: $300 million, $2.5 billion, $50 thousand
  • Abbreviated amounts: $100k, $5M, $2B
  • USD written: USD 1.5 million, USD 500 thousand
  • Dollars written: 2.3 billion dollars, 750 thousand dollars
